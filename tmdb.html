Introduction¶

Welcome to this exploration of Tmdb dataset. this dataset contains 10000 movie from year 1960 to year 2015. we will discover this data & will make the process needed (wrangling & cleaning) to answer some questions. We will be answering :
1- Which genres aremost popular fromyear to year?
2- What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?
Also, we will explore conitribution of actors, directors & production companies in the most movies.

Conclusions¶

Limitations¶

  • Some columns needed much time to modify by splitting such as cast, production companies & director. I had to get a copy of the data when splitting.
  • popularity is a subjective value because it was collected from users on different websites. also, votes collected in the same way. this may lead to less accurate results.
